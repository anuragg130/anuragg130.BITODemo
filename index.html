    ⚙ Step 1 of 8

    Eligibility Gates

    These are mandatory pass/fail checks. A “No” answer to any gate will disqualify the application at this stage. Please answer honestly.

    E1 — Is your entity legally registered in India?
    Includes Pvt Ltd, LLP, Partnership, Cooperative, FPO, SHG, Proprietorship, or any entity incorporated under Indian law.
    E2 — Is food processing or food manufacturing the primary business activity of your entity?
    Excludes primary agriculture/farming, livestock, fisheries without value addition, and packaged drinking water (as per BITO norms).
    E3 — Do Indian nationals hold 51% or more ownership in your entity?
    Applicable to all entity types. For FPOs, cooperatives and SHGs, this criterion is considered automatically satisfied.
    E4 — Has your entity received monetary grant support exceeding ₹50 lakh in aggregate from any Central or State Government scheme in the current financial year?
    If yes, the application will be flagged for review but will not be automatically disqualified. Subsidies, credit-linked capital support, and equity investments are treated separately.

    📎 Proof of Bihar OperationsMandatory
